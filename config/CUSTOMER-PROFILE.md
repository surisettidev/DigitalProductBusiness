# Customer Profile

## Primary persona — "Rahul, the stuck freelancer"
- 26 years old, lives in tier-1 or tier-2 Indian city
- 2–4 years of freelancing experience
- Earns ₹40k–₹1.2L/month, wildly inconsistent
- Skills: writing, design, web dev, video editing, marketing
- Uses Upwork/Fiverr/LinkedIn, increasingly direct clients
- Spends 1–2 hrs/day on Reddit + X
- Comfortable spending ₹500–₹2,000 on a useful guide if it solves a real problem

## Top 5 pains
1. "I don't know if I'm undercharging" (pricing anxiety)
2. "Months are feast or famine" (client pipeline)
3. "Everyone says use AI but I waste hours figuring out which tool" (AI overwhelm)
4. "GST and taxes confuse me" (compliance anxiety)
5. "I'm working 12 hours and still feel disorganized" (operations)

## Buying triggers
- Just raised rates and lost a client → wants pricing validation
- Hit ₹20L turnover → suddenly GST anxiety
- New AI tool launched → wants curated guidance
- Burned by an Upwork client → ready to invest in direct outreach
