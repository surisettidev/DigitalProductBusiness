# Brand Voice Guide

## Tone
- Practical > theoretical
- Direct > polite-corporate
- Specific > generic
- Indian context > generic global

## Always
- Use INR (₹), GST, ONDC, UPI references where relevant
- Mention concrete numbers (hours saved, ₹ earned)
- Use second person ("you", "your")
- Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Plain English.

## Never
- Use words like "synergy", "leverage", "ideate", "circle back"
- Make claims you can't back up
- Use generic stock-photo language ("game-changer", "next-level")
- Promise specific income outcomes ("earn ₹1L in 30 days")

## Voice samples (GOOD)
- "Most freelancers undercharge because they price their time, not the client's outcome."
- "₹699 to stop wasting 8 hours/week picking the wrong AI tool. That's ~₹85/hour saved if you save just 1 hour."
- "GST registration takes 3 days online, costs ₹0, and you only need it past ₹20 lakh turnover."

## Voice samples (BAD — never write like this)
- "Unlock your true potential with our revolutionary system!"
- "Join thousands of successful freelancers..."
- "Game-changing strategies that will transform your business..."
