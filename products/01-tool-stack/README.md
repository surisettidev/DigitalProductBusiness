## Why this product exists

Indian freelancers waste 40+ hours researching tools when starting out. ChatGPT gives generic global lists. This is the **opinionated, India-tested** shortcut.

## Buyer

- Indian freelancer 0–3 years in
- ₹20K–₹2L/mo earner
- Uses 5+ apps already but suspects they're paying for things they don't need

## Outcome

After 1 hour with this guide, the buyer:
- knows the exact stack to use for invoicing, payments, GST, project mgmt, contracts, AI
- saved ₹2,000+/month on overlapping subscriptions
- has GST-compliant invoice template ready to send
